# Resign with a Laugh: Your Personal Letter Writing Assistant

## Design Goal
The goal of this application is to help people write resignation letters that are funny, unique, and tell a weird little story. Quitting a job can be stressful, so this app makes it a fun and collaborative experience. It uses a team of virtual "agents" (like a Project Manager, Writer, and Editor) to create the perfect letter for you.

## How It Works
When you type a message, you are talking to the **Project Manager**. The Project Manager takes your ideas and secretly passes them down an assembly line of distinct, specialized AI agents:
1. **Project Manager**: Evaluates your request, writes a "Project Brief", and breaks it down into specific instructions for the team.
2. **Researcher**: Gathers facts, themes, and weird storytelling ideas based on the instructions.
3. **Writer**: Takes the research and the brief to write the first draft of the funny story.
4. **Editor**: Fixes spelling, grammar, and sentence flow.
5. **Graphics/UX/UI Designer**: Formats the letter so it looks good on the page.
6. **Ethics Reviewer**: Simultaneously checks the letter to make sure the joke doesn't go too far and stays safe.

The app has two main parts on the screen:
1. **The Chat**: On the left, where you talk to the Project Manager. You will see live updates showing exactly which agent is currently working on your letter!
2. **The Document**: On the right, which looks like a piece of paper where your letter magically appears and updates as you chat.

## Glossary
- **Agent**: A virtual helper with a specific job (like a virtual writer or editor). In this app, each role is a separate, distinct call to the AI.
- **Prompt**: The instructions or ideas you type into the chat.
- **Iterate**: To make changes and improvements over and over until it is perfect.
- **Mockup**: A visual representation of what something will look like in real life (in this case, a piece of paper).
- **UX/UI**: User Experience and User Interface. This just means how the app looks and how easy it is to use.
